# HM15211, a novel long-acting GLP-1/GIP/Glucagon tri-agonist

Hanmi logo

Diagram of HM15211 structure showing GLP-1/GIP/GCG triple agonist, Flexible PEG Linker, and Aglycosylated Fc fragment

LAPSCOVERY : Long Acting Peptide/Protein DiSCOVERY Technology

**Hanmi’s GLP-1/GIP/GCG triple agonist is conjugated with a human IgG Fc fragment via flexible linker**

**[General profiles]**

* High glucagon activity, suitable for liver preferential distribution and NASH treatment

* Balanced GLP-1 and GIP action to neutralize hyperglycemic risk of high GCG

* Improved lipid profile, and anti-inflammatory effect by new born GIP

* Similar intrinsic activity profile between human and rodent receptor

* Extended half life (t<sub>1/2</sub> = 42.7 ~ 55 hr in mice; 82.8 ~ 85.7 hr in rats)

* Good Solubility (≥ 150 mg/mL) & bioavailability (≥ 95 %)

# Summary & Conclusion

Hanmi logo

* HM15211 is a novel long-acting triple agonist possessing high GCG and balanced GLP-1 and GIP activity

* High GCG activity makes HM15211 be preferentially distributed to liver

<u>*In NASH animal models*</u>

* Together with food intake regulation, enhanced energy expenditure via browning of WAT explains potent body weight loss efficacy of HM15211

* Consistently, HM15211 reduces hepatic TG contents

With unique GLP-1/GIP/GCG activity, HM15211 might provide favorable therapeutic efficacy than existing therapeutics for the treatment of NASH as well as obesity
