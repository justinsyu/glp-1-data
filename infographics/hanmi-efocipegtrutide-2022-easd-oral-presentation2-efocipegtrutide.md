<section>
  <h2>Source Conclusions</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>HM15211 is described as a novel long-acting glucagon/GIP/GLP-1 triple agonist with therapeutic potential demonstrated in animal models of NASH and fibrosis.</li>
    <li>The source says HM15211 was readily distributed to lung in addition to liver, supporting investigation in fibrotic lung disease such as IPF.</li>
    <li>In BLM mice, HM15211 improved pulmonary respiratory function and survival rate.</li>
    <li>The presentation concludes that HM15211 might be a potential therapeutic option for IPF in addition to NASH, while human studies are required for human relevance.</li>
  </ul>
