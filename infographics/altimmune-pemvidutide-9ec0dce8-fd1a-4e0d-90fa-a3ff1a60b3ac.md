  <h2>Study At A Glance</h2>
  <div class="flow-diagram" aria-label="Study design">
    <div>Clinicaltrials.gov# NCT05295875</div>
    <div>Phase 2, 48-week trial</div>
    <div>Randomized 1:1:1:1 to placebo, 1.2 mg, 1.8 mg, or 2.4 mg weekly</div>
    <div>Body composition MRIs at baseline and Week 48</div>
  </div>
  <div class="evidence-grid evidence-grid-2">
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Eligibility</h3>
      <p>Men and women, ages 18-65 years, with BMI &ge; 30 kg/m2 or BMI &ge; 27 kg/m2 with at least one obesity-related comorbidity, HbA1c &le; 6.5%, fasting glucose &le; 125 mg/dL, at least one unsuccessful weight loss attempt, and a minimum of ~ 25% of subjects were to be male.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Outcome Measures</h3>
      <p>Lean mass, visceral adipose tissue (VAT), subcutaneous adipose tissue (SAT), and total adipose tissue (TAT) were quantified at baseline and after 48 weeks of pemvidutide treatment.</p>
