  <h2>Study at a glance</h2>
  <div class="flow-diagram" aria-label="HRS9531 study design">
    <div>249 enrolled</div>
    <div>248 received at least one post-randomization dose</div>
    <div>24-week core period</div>
    <div>28-week extension</div>
    <div>154 completed extension treatment</div>
  </div>
  <div class="callout">
    <strong>Population and dosing</strong>
    <p>Chinese adults with BMI 28 to 40 kg/m2 were randomized 1:1:1:1:1 to once-weekly subcutaneous HRS9531 1.0, 3.0, 4.5, or 6.0 mg, or placebo, during the 24-week core period, followed by a 28-week extension.</p>

<section>
  <h2>Extension phase</h2>
  <div class="evidence-grid evidence-grid-2">
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Weight maintenance</h3>
      <p>From Week 32 to Week 52, mean percentage change in body weight ranged from -0.76% to 0.01% in HRS9531 every-two-week groups versus -3.45% to -0.02% in HRS9531 weekly groups.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Dosing interpretation</h3>
      <p>The source states that the every-two-week regimen maintained weight stability, suggesting that less frequent dosing may be adequate for long-term weight loss maintenance.</p>
    </article>
  </div>
</section>
