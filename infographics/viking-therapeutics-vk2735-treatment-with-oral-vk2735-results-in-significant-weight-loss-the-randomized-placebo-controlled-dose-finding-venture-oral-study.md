<section>
  <h2>Study At A Glance</h2>
  <div class="flow-diagram" aria-label="VENTURE-Oral study design">
    <div>Phase 2, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled dose-finding study</div>
    <div>Adults with BMI >=30 kg/m<sup>2</sup>, or BMI >=27 kg/m<sup>2</sup> with at least one weight-related comorbidity</div>
    <div>Once-daily oral VK2735 or placebo for 13 weeks</div>
    <div>Primary endpoint: percent change in body weight from baseline to Week 13 in the mITT population</div>
    <div>Secondary and exploratory endpoints included safety and additional efficacy measures</div>
