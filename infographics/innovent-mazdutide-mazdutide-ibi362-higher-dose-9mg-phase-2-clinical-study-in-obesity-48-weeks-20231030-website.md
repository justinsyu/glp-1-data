<section>
  <h2>Safety Profile</h2>
  <div class="evidence-grid evidence-grid-2">
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Overall AE profile</h3>
      <p>Gastrointestinal adverse reactions, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, were the most common adverse events and were mostly mild or moderate.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>No SAE</h3>
      <p>No subject in the mazdutide group discontinued treatment due to adverse events, and no serious adverse events occurred.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Cardiovascular profile</h3>
      <p>The source states heart-rate increase in the mazdutide group was similar to placebo after 24 weeks, with no further increase during extension.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Risk signal</h3>
      <p>No signal of increased cardiovascular risk was observed throughout the treatment period.</p>
    </article>
  </div>
</section>

  <div class="callout">
    <strong>Clinical update summary</strong>
    <p>Innovent summarized mazdutide 9 mg as showing robust weight loss, differentiated cardiometabolic benefits, a favorable safety profile, no adverse event led discontinuation in the mazdutide group, and a simple two-step titration regimen.</p>
