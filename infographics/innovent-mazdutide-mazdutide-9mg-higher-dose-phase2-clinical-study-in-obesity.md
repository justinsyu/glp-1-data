<section>
  <h2>Safety Snapshot</h2>
  <div class="evidence-grid evidence-grid-2">
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Adverse events</h3>
      <p>Except for COVID-19 infection, the most commonly reported adverse events were gastrointestinal related and were mostly mild or moderate and transient.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Discontinuation</h3>
      <p>The source states the mazdutide group drop-out rate was lower than placebo and that no mazdutide subject discontinued treatment due to adverse events.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Serious adverse events</h3>
      <p>No serious adverse events occurred in the source's Week 24 safety summary.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Data maturity</h3>
      <p>The deck notes that full primary, secondary, exploratory, and safety analyses were to be disclosed after study completion.</p>
    </article>
