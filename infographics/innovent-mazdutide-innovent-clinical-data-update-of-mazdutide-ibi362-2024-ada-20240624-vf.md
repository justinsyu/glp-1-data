<section>
  <h2>Safety Profile</h2>
  <div class="evidence-grid evidence-grid-2">
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Adverse events</h3>
      <p>Gastrointestinal symptoms, including nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea, were the most common adverse events and were mostly mild or moderate in severity.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="evidence-card">
      <h3>Heart rate</h3>
      <p>The deck states mean heart-rate changes from baseline were no more than 5 beats/min throughout 48 weeks for both mazdutide groups, with no observed safety signal of increased cardiovascular risk.</p>
    </article>
