<section>
  <h2>Source Conclusions</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>HM15211, a glucagon/GIP/GLP-1 triple agonist, is designed to treat NASH and fibrosis by targeting multiple aspects of the disease, in clinical phase 2.</li>
    <li>In the intratracheal BLM instilled IPF model, HM15211 improved respiratory functional impairment and subsequently mortality.</li>
    <li>HM15211 also ameliorated sub-pleural fibrosis and respiratory function in subcutaneously infused BLM induced lung fibrosis mice.</li>
    <li>HM15211 has therapeutic potential for IPF with improved efficacy over the current standard of care in several nonclinical studies.</li>
  </ul>
