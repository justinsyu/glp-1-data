# <u>Mazdutide special liver benefits</u>

## Directly targets liver, which plays a central role in metabolic diseases

```mermaid
graph LR
    subgraph Mazdutide_Action [Mazdutide]
        M[MazdutideGLP-1R/GCGR Dual Agonist] --> GLP[GLP-1 receptor]
        M --> GCGR[Glucagon receptor]
    end

    subgraph Liver_Dysfunction [Liver metabolic dysfunction]
        L1[MASLD/MASH]
        L2[Hyperuricaemia]
        L3[Hypercholesterolemia]
        L4[Hypertriglyceridemia]
    end

    Mazdutide_Action --> Liver_Dysfunction
    Liver_Dysfunction --> CI[Chronic inflammation]

    CI --> T2D[T2D]
    CI --> CVD[Cardiovascular diseases]
    CI --> CKD[Chronic kidney disease]
```

Liver plays a central role in human metabolism. Its dysfunction can lead to chronic inflammation, which underlies the pathogenesis of various metabolic diseases such as **T2D**, **cardiovascular diseases**, **chronic kidney diseases**.

MASLD=metabolic dysfunction-associated steatotic liver disease; MASH=metabolic dysfunction-associated steatohepatitis； T2D= type 2 diabetes

Dose regimen diagram showing three arms: Mazdutide 4mg (titrated from 2mg), Mazdutide 6mg (titrated from 2mg and 4mg), and Placebo. Screening starts at week -2, randomization 1:1:1 at week 0, primary endpoint at week 32, end of treatment at week 48, and follow-up until week 60.
